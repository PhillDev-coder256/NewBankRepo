ABOUT US

TAIM AFRICA is a registered non-profit arts-motivation movement in Uganda (East Africa) with a ground mission of discovering, nurturing and platforming teenage amd early-youth creative arts alongside sustaining their academic lives. The movement exixts in youth communities and secondary / high schools as it fetches-out passionate creative drammers in the arts industry and molds them great persons of influence.

The organization started in 2018 as aprogram of a copyright protection company AICODE(Alliance of Independent Copyright Defenders) in Uganda where it was a child film acting mentorship initiative. In 2019, the by then program administrators took a step to register the program separate from the company [AICODE] to an independent non-profit organization.

Since then, the organization has grown bold and independent with approved registration certificates from the Uganda government.

OBJECTIVES

  • To influence policy and practice of film-talent development and management
  • To represent the views of those involved in film industry development
  • To support the film sector in increasing and sustaining participation levels
  • To create a profiting atmosphere for film practitioners
  • To create a joint and cooperative ground for the film industry
  • To facilitate capacity building for nurturing and developing of film talents
